The Story

Rock & Roll Soul

The Backstage Bowl isn't just a food truck—it's a concert experience on wheels. We believe food should have energy, personality, and soul.

Every soup on our menu is named after songs that move us, inspire us, and make us want to turn up the volume. From classic rock to contemporary hits, our menu celebrates the music that soundtracks our lives.

Operating across St. Johns and surrounding counties, we're bringing gourmet soup experiences to neighborhoods everywhere. Crafted with premium ingredients and served with rock-solid passion.

Turn up the bowl. Experience the difference when food meets rock and roll.
